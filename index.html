About Me

I invested my time into studying history and economics and eventually found myself brewing professionally. The older I have become, the more I see that computers, and understanding the technologies associated with them, is necessary and often fun.

I am now beginning a journey that will allow me to make a difference in the surrounding environment. I will be able to help shape the direction future technologies are going and maybe get others interested in this dynamic and emerging field.
